Essential Acne Treatment Guidelines

Following these essential acne guidelines will ensure your skin clearing proceeds properly and maintains the benefits of your treatment sessions. These recommendations are based on Dr. Vaishali Sapat's extensive experience and proven clinical protocols. From immediate pre-treatment preparation to long-term maintenance, these guidelines will help you achieve optimal results while preventing complications and enhancing your skin's natural healing process.

  • Maintain proper facial hygiene using gentle, non-comedogenic cleansers recommended by our clinic specialists
  • Follow prescribed topical medications and oral supplements as directed to promote optimal acne recovery
  • Avoid excessive touching and use non-irritating moisturizers daily for skin barrier protection
  • Refrain from picking, squeezing, or popping breakouts to prevent scarring damage and infection
  • Attend all scheduled acne sessions and follow-up appointments for progress monitoring and assessment

These essential acne guidelines ensure optimal skin clearing and long-lasting results from your treatments. Contact SkinAyurveda for personalized acne instructions and ongoing support.
